Palak Rice

palak Rice is one of the easiest and healthiest rice recipe out of all the flavored rice recipe. there are several ways of preparing the palak rice recipe, but i am sharing the easiest recipe with cooked rice. this palak recipe is ideal for tiffin boxes to be prepare in the busy mornings or even for breakfast.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

4 servings

instructions

step 1

firstly, in wide pan, take a tsp of ghee. optionally, you can use oil.

step 2

add chopped onions and green chilli. fry till they turn translucent.

step 3

time to add finely chopped palak leaves and fry for a minute till they shrink and change its colour.

step 4

once the palak leaves are cooked, add peas and fry till the peas are cooked.

step 5

finally, add cooked rice and salt to taste.

step 6

mix well without breaking rice grains. cook for a minute.

step 7

finally, serve palak pulao with raita or as it is.

ingredients

1 cup basmati rice (cooked)

1 tsp ghee / clarified butter

¼ onion (thinly sliced)

2 green chilli (slit length wise)

1 cup palak leaves / spinach (finely chopped)

¼ cup peas (fresh / frozen)

salt to taste

bowls
