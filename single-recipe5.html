Bisi Bele Bath

Bisi Bele Bath is one of the most common rice based lunch box recipes prepared in almost every house hold in south india,it is prepared for either breakfast,lunch/even a tiffin box, it can also be prepared for dinner with the choise of raita

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

4 servings

instructions

step 1

firstly add Toor Dal,Rice,Turmeric,oil,3 cup of water to the pressure cooker,
add oil,ghee,mustard, dried chilli,hing,peanuts,onions,carrot,potato,peas,beans,tomato and curry leaves in tawa and heat and ,heat until the vegetables soften slightly.

step 2

add tamarind extract and boil,bisibele bath malsala,chilli powder and salt

step 3

Saute on Low Flame Until The Spices Get Aromatic

step 4

add cooked rice-dal ,salt,water to the Tawa

step 5

add fried cashew,ghee and saute until it turn aromatic

ingredients

1/2 cup of Toor Dal

1/2 cup of Rice

3 cup of Water

2 tbsp Oil

2 tbsp of Ghee

1 tbsp of Mustard

2 dried Chilli

pinch of hing and 2 tbsp of Peanuts

Few Curry Leaves

1/2 Onion
1 Carrot
1 Potato
1/2 Tomato

5 Beans
2 tbsp of Peas
Chilli Powder

1/2 cup Tamarind Extract

Bisi Bele Bath Masala

2 tbsp Cashew
