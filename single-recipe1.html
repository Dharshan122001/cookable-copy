Veg Pualo

Veg pulao is comfort food at its best and I make it when I want to cook something quick, easy and satisfying. This one-pot dish made with rice and vegetables has added spices and herbs, giving it a mild aromatic flavor. This special recipe is my mom’s and is my go-to recipe when making veg pulao. The dish is also vegetarian and is great on its own or paired with raita (an Indian yogurt dish), pickle and roasted papad (a crisp thin Indian snack).

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

4 servings

instructions

step 1

Firstly, in a small mixi take a given Coriander,Mint,Ginger,Garlic,Chilli,Cardamom,Cinnamon and water and blend it to a smoot Paste

step 2

In a Large Kadai,Heat Ghee Add Cumin,BayLeaf,Cardamom,Cinnamon,Cloves,Pepper And cashew

step 3

Saute on Low Flame Until The Spices Get Aromatic

step 4

Add Onion And saute Until The Color Changes Slightly Add Tomato And Saute Until The Tomatoes Turn Soft And Mushy Add Beans ,Capsicum,Peas,Carrot and Potato And Saute For a Minute Until it Smells Aromatic

step 5

Add Prepared Masala to It And Pour Water and Salt and Wait until It bolis Then Add Rice To It

ingredients

2 Cup Wheat Flour

Half tbsp Salt

Water

2 tbsp Oil

3 Potato

Ginger Paste

2 Chilli

2 tbsp Coriander

1/2 tbsp Coriander Seeds

1 tbsp Chilli Powder

1/2 tbsp Cumin Powder

1 1/2 cups dry pancake mix

Garam Masala

Aamchur
