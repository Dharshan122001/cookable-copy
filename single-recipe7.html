Jeera Rice

Jeera Rice is a easy and flavoured rice recipe made with cumin seeds, ghee and basmati rice. it is typically served with choice of dal recipes, preferably dal tadka recipe, but can be served with any gravy based curries.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

4 servings

instructions

step 1

firstly, in a cooker heat 1 tsp ghee and splutter 1 tsp cumin seeds.

step 2

now add ½ cup basmati rice (soaked 20 minutes) and saute for a minute.

step 3

add 1 cup of water, ¼ tsp salt and mix well.

step 4

cover and pressure cook for 2 whistles.

step 5

finally, add 2 tbsp coriander leaves and enjoy jeera rice with dal.

ingredients

1 tsp ghee / clarified butter

1 tsp cumin / jeera

½ cup basmati rice

1 cup water

¼ tsp salt

2 tbsp coriander (finely chopped)
