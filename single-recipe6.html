Masala Dosa

Masala Dosa is an extremely popular, simple, easy and instant dosa recipe prepared with semolina & potato bhaji. it is basically a quick and cheat version of the popular traditional masala dosa recipe which is prepared with rice and urad dal dosa batter. it can be an ideal morning breakfast meal recipe, if not for evening snacks and can be served with a choice of chutney and multipurpose sambar.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

4 servings

instructions

step 1

firstly, pour a ladleful of batter on hot tawa. spread slightly thin to make crispy dosa.

step 2

spread some oil and butter uniformly. also, place 2 tbsp of prepared aloo masala in the centre. roast until the dosa turns golden brown and crisp.

step 3

scrape the sides of the dosa and fold half. finally, rava masala dosa recipe is ready to serve with coconut chutney

ingredients

Dosa Batter

Aaloo Stuff

4 tbsp of Ghee/Oil

2 cup of Chutney (if Required)

tools

plates

bowls

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
