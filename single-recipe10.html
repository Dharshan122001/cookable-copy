Dal Kichdi

Dal Kichdi is one of the health and comfort food recipe made with a combination of rice and moong dal. across india, khichdi recipes are made for different reasons and occasions, but the basic ingredients and combination remains the same. generally, it has the all spices and flavours in it and is served as it is without any sides, but tastes great when served with a hint of pickle or creamy yoghurt.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

4 servings

instructions

step 1

firstly, in a large bowl rinse and soak ½ cup rice, ½ cup moong dal for 10 minutes.
in a cooker heat 1 tsp ghee and add soaked dal and rice.

step 2

now add ¼ tsp turmeric, ½ tsp salt and 3¼ cup water. mix well.

step 3

cover and pressure cook for 5 minutes.
in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp ghee.
add 1 tsp cumin, 1 bay leaf and pinch hing.

step 4

saute on low flame until the spices turn aromatic.
now add 1 onion, 1 tsp ginger garlic paste and saute well.

step 5

further, add 1 tomato and saute until the tomato turns soft and mushy.
keeping the flame on low add ¼ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chilli powder, ½ tsp garam masala and ½ tsp salt.

step 6

saute for 2 minutes or until the spices turn aromatic.
now add cooked rice and dal.

step 7

also, add 1 cup water and mix well adjusting consistency as required.
cover and simmer for 5 minutes or until the flavours are absorbed well.

ingredients

2 Cup Wheat Flour

½ cup rice

1 tsp ghee

¼ tsp turmeric

½ tsp salt

1 tsp cumin

1 bay leaf

pinch hing

1 onion (finely chopped)

1 tsp ginger garlic paste

1 tomato (finely chopped)

½ tsp garam masala

2 tbsp coriander (finely chopped)

½ cup moong dal
