veg Fried Rice

This classic fried rice recipe is loaded with fresh mixed veggies and aromatic spices for an incredibly hearty, flavorful vegan dish. Hands-down this is one of our favorite Chinese-inspired recipes. Not only is vegetable fried rice amazingly easy to make from scratch, it’s a naturally vegan dish that’s as good for you as it is delicious to eat. You can even tweak veg fried rice to be gluten-free simply by using GF-friendly soy sauce or tamari.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

4 servings

instructions

step 1

Firstly,in a large Kadai Heat 2 tbsp Oil and Stir-Fry 2 Clove Garlic.

step 2

Add Onion And saute Until The Color Changes Slightly
Add Beans ,Capsicum,Peas,Carrot and Potato And Saute For a Minute Until it Smells Aromatic
Add Prepared Masala to It And Pour Water and Salt and Wait until It boils

step 3

Now Add Soya Sauce And Vinegar and Combine Well
Saute on Low Flame Until The Spices Get Aromatic

step 4

Then Add Soaked Rice To It

ingredients

  1. 1 cup Basmati Rice Soaked
  2. 1 tbsp Oil
  3. half cup Salt
  4. Sliced Onion
  5. Garlic Paste
  6. Finely Chopped Beans
  7. Cubed Capsicum
  8. Peas
  9. Chopped Carrot
  10. Potato
  11. Pepper
  12. Vinegar
  13. Soya Sauce
