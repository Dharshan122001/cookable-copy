Aaloo Paratha

Aaloo Paratha are indian flat breads stuffed with delicious spiced potato mixture,in hindi Aaloo means "potato" paratha means "flat bread"

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

4 servings

instructions

step 1

In A Large Bowl Take 2 Cup Of Wheat Flour and Half tbsp Salt and Mix Well

step 2

Add Water as Required And Start To Knead
Add 2 tbsp Of Oil And Knead Well
Grease Oil And Rest For 20 Minutes

step 3

Take Potato,Ginger Paste,Chilli,Coriander and its seeds
Add Chilli Powder,Cumin Powder,Garam Masala,Aamchur And Salt

step 4

Mix Well And Make Sure All The Ingredients Are Well Combined. Aloo Stuffing Is Ready

step 5

After Dough Has Rested For 20 Mins ,Knead Slightly
Pinch A Ball Of Stuff And Roll It and Place A Ball Sized Aloo Stuff in The Center Close Around the Stuff

step 6

Sprinkle Some Wheat Flour And Roll Slightly Thick

step 7

On A Hot Tawa Place a Rolled Paratha And Cook For A Minute
Flip The Paratha When The Base Is Partly Cooked

step 8

Brush Oil And Ghee TO Both Sides Of Paratha While Flipping
Serve Hot Aloo Paratha With The Given Sauce/Raita

ingredients

2 Cup Wheat Flour

Half tbsp Salt

Water

2 tbsp Oil

3 Potato

Ginger Paste

2 Chilli

2 tbsp Coriander

1/2 tbsp Coriander Seeds

1 tbsp Chilli Powder

1/2 tbsp Cumin Powder

1 1/2 cups dry pancake mix

Garam Masala

Aamchur
