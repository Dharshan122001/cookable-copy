Tomato Bath

Tomato bath is a savory, tangy and flavorful rice-based dish made with a ground paste of tomatoes, coconut, spices and herbs. The addition of this ground masala paste takes this dish to another level. My recipe is of Karnataka-style tomato rice that is popularly known as tomato bath. Here the word ‘bath’ translates to ‘rice’.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

4 servings

instructions

step 1

In a Large Kadai,Heat oil Add Cumin,BayLeaf,Cardamom,Cinnamon,Cloves,Pepper And cashew

step 2

Saute on Low Flame Until The Spices Get Aromatic

step 3

Saute on Low Flame Until The Spices Get Aromatic

step 4

Add Onion And saute Until The Color Changes Slightly
Add Tomato And Saute Until The Tomatoes Turn Soft And Mushy
Add Turmeric,Chilli Powder,Coriander Powder,Salt and Mint Leaves

step 5

Add Beans ,Capsicum,Peas,Carrot And Saute For a Minute Until it Smells Aromatic
Add Prepared Masala to It And Pour Water and Salt and Wait until It bolis
Then Add Rice To It
